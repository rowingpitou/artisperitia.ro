This year's workshop saw a feminin team challenge the transformation of the old closets in a cozy house for the future craftsman of Curtisoara. And with a perpetual smile!

Of course, the traditional special locals day was again a success with all our young friends from Curtisoara joining during the day to share games and activities together in this great museum.
