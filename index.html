The 2015 Workshop organised by Artis Peritia this summer is scheduled between July 20th and August 3rd. (info)

We have many things to do this year!

  • restoration of the old wooden shingle roof of the Conac
  • restoration of the zinc roof of the “farmer’s house”
  • Furniture construction and interior finishing in the Compagnon’s house
  • restoration of the Conac windows (carpentry)
  • if the weather allows it, strawbale filling and clay plastering of the walls of the future working place.
  • landscaping of the parc

Don't hesitate to contact us if you have any questions (artisperitia@gmail.com).
